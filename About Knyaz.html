About Knyaz USA


Knyaz club is an international HMB fighting team that has been growing over the past 10 years. "Knyaz" refers to the historical Slavic titles for a prince, duke or count. Not only does this give homage to their roots but it also instills the clubs mindset, that is, to become princes of the battlefield. Knyaz takes part in many HMB competitions and festivals across the globe including "Battle of the Nations". Training and fighting with Kynaz not only focuses on physical development but also mental strength. Fighters learn about medieval culture and the historical importance of medieval battle, tactics and arms. As the sport continues to grow so does Knyaz, they now have multiple branches including teams in Ukraine, UK and Italy.

 

While the fighters wearing the “praise the sun” tabards are not new to the sport, Knyaz USA is a new and up and coming team in HMB having debuted at Carolina Carnage in 2021. Based out of Armada, Michigan the team comes from the larger part of the Detroit area and stretches all the way to the west side of the state, in Grand Rapids, Michigan. Today, that idea is reality, and Knyaz USA is bringing the storm!

 

Knyaz USA fights with a mentality to “hit hard and move fast.” Spending a lot of time training each individual fighter to ensure every link in the chain is just as strong as the last. They fight as a team, and this strong focus on unity gives them an advantage in their fights. However, Knyaz USA stresses on all in their ranks that they will not enter a fight to win by harming others intentionally, but instead they will win their victories honorably and with dignity.

 

If this sounds like a team you would like to be a part of, Knyaz USA would be happy to have you. All are welcome, from novice to veteran!
